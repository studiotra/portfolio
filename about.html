Behind Studiotra

I’m Petra — Designing Systems, Not Just Assets

Brand Design Lead, Creative Director, and Front-End Builder based in Toronto

I design brand systems that scale. From investor decks to marketing toolkits, cross-channel campaigns, and video templates, I create frameworks that empower teams — not just designers — to produce polished, consistent work quickly. My focus is on clarity, repeatability, and operational impact, turning complex information into structured, reusable design systems that actually work for real teams.

Over the past 11+ years, I've worked across startups, enterprise clients, and everything in between, building visual systems, modular templates, and workflows that simplify production and maintain brand consistency. I thrive on projects that challenge me to think beyond individual assets, designing systems that make teams faster, smarter, and more effective.
