Service

Client

Amazon

Industry

Tech / Advertising

Objective

Cross-Channel Marketing Asset Development

Role

Graphic Designer

Overview

Developed a comprehensive marketing toolkit to support sales and marketing teams promoting Amazon Ads solutions to enterprise advertisers. The project focused on creating a scalable set of templates and visual assets that could be reused across presentations, case studies, and marketing materials while maintaining brand consistency.

Challenge

The marketing team needed a flexible system to communicate product value, advertising capabilities, and campaign success stories across multiple channels. Existing materials were fragmented and difficult to adapt for different audiences, resulting in duplicated design effort and inconsistent messaging.

The goal was to create a unified toolkit that would allow teams to quickly assemble polished marketing assets without requiring constant design support.

Approach

I designed a modular toolkit that established clear visual patterns for marketing storytelling, data presentation, and product messaging. The system prioritized clarity and adaptability so teams could build new materials quickly while preserving brand integrity.

Key design principles included:

  • Strong information hierarchy for complex advertising concepts
  • Flexible layouts for different content lengths and campaign needs
  • Visual components optimized for presentations and digital distribution

The toolkit was developed through collaboration with marketing stakeholders to ensure the templates aligned with real sales and campaign workflows.

Design System

The toolkit included a set of reusable components that enabled marketing teams to build materials efficiently:

  • Presentation templates for product overviews and case studies
  • Data visualization layouts for advertising metrics and performance
  • Modular slide patterns for campaign storytelling
  • Branded visual components including typography, icons, and layout grids

These elements created a consistent visual language that allowed new marketing assets to be produced quickly without compromising design quality.

Outcome & Impact

The final toolkit provided a scalable framework that streamlined the production of marketing materials for sales and campaign communications.

By introducing reusable templates and standardized layouts, the system improved visual consistency across assets and reduced the need for repeated design work while enabling teams to generate polished presentations and marketing materials more independently.
