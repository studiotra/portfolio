AmazonAds × Fizz

Video templates and cross-channel assets that streamline case study production for marketing teams.

Client:
Amazon

Service:
Video Template System, Marketing Enablement Toolkit

Industry:
Tech / Advertising

Template Design · Video · Cross-Channel Systems

Hero image

Context / Problem

Amazon's marketing team needed a fast, consistent way to produce case study videos. Designers were manually creating each video, which slowed down production and introduced inconsistency.

My Role & Approach

I built a video template system allowing designers to quickly populate case study videos. The templates included reusable sequences, animation rules, and brand-consistent visual elements.

Solution Overview

  • Modular video components (intro, metrics, testimonials, outro)
  • Brand-consistent typography, color, and motion rules
  • Editable sequences for designer efficiency
  • Cross-channel-ready for social, presentations, and website

Impact & Results

  • Templates used in recent case study videos
  • Streamlined production and reduced design time
  • Ensured brand consistency across all video assets

Key Highlights

Shows system thinking for non-designer teams, cross-channel enablement, and operational scalability in a video context.
