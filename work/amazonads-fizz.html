AmazonAds X Fizz Case Study Motion Template

Service

Video Template System, Marketing Enablement

Client

Amazon

Industry

Tech / Advertising

Objective

Cross-Channel Marketing Asset Development

Role

Lead Designer

Overview

Worked with the client and internal marketing team at Amazon Ads to design a modular motion system for case study videos and campaign cutdowns. The goal was to replace a slow, manual production workflow with a repeatable system that designers and content teams could use to generate on-brand videos across channels (summit presentations, web, and social).

Challenge

Marketing needed a fast, consistent way to produce compelling case-study videos for product launches and events. Each video previously required full custom motion work, which introduced:

  • Inconsistent visual language across assets
  • Long turnaround times for event deliverables
  • High dependency on senior motion designers for routine edits

Approach

I led the design and delivery of a modular motion framework focused on clarity, brand fidelity, and repeatability. Key activities:

  • Mapped existing video production workflows and identified repeatable moments (intro, hero metrics, testimonial, product demo, outro)
  • Defined a motion language and animation rules aligned to the new brand identity
  • Built editable Premiere/After Effects template sequences and exported cross-channel masters for social, site, and presentations
  • Worked closely with the client's content producers to test templates on real assets and iterate for usability

Design System / Key Contribution

Instead of delivering a single piece of motion, I delivered a system that marketing and designers could reuse:

  • Modular sequences — Intro, context, data/metrics, testimonial, CTA, outro; each sequence built as an editable template
  • Animation rules & tokens — Consistent timing, easing, and scale rules so motion feels cohesive across cutdowns
  • Brand components — Reusable title bars, lower thirds, data charts, and image masks that adhere to brand typography and color
  • Cross-channel exports — Preset aspect ratios and compression settings for web, LinkedIn, and Instagram
  • Designer enablement — Quick-start documentation + a short demo that allowed junior designers to produce edits without full motion training

Outcome & Impact

Replaced ad-hoc, fully custom video production with a repeatable system that improved consistency across case study assets.

Templates were used for summit materials and social cutdowns, reducing routine video edit overhead and lowering review iterations.

Marketing teams gained the ability to produce timely, on-brand video content for product and sales enablement with less reliance on senior motion resources.
