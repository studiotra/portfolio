PE/RE Investor Deck

Scalable investor deck templates that empower non-designers and streamline B2B communications.

Client:
Confidential / B2B Client

Service:
Presentation Design, Template System

Industry:
Finance / Investment

Branding · Template Design · Systems

Hero image

Context / Problem

Investor decks were inconsistent, lacked structure, and were difficult to update. Teams had to manually recreate slides, resulting in slow turnaround, brand drift, and difficulty communicating complex financial information.

My Role & Approach

Led the redesign and creation of a scalable template system. Responsibilities included auditing decks, defining content hierarchy, building master slides and components, and creating usage guidelines for non-designers.

Solution Overview

  • Modular master slides and components
  • Standardized typography, spacing, and chart layouts
  • Clear visual hierarchy for investor readability
  • Editable templates for non-designers

Impact & Results

  • Reduced production time dramatically
  • Enabled three investment teams to produce decks independently
  • Ensured brand consistency and clarity for investors

Key Highlights

Demonstrates B2B system thinking, scalable templates, and non-designer enablement.
