TD Financial Literacy

Scalable educational video system ensuring brand consistency across financial literacy campaigns.

Client:
TD Bank

Service:
Video Production, Content System

Industry:
Financial Services / Education

Video · Systems · Client Education

Hero image

Context / Problem

Educational videos were produced ad hoc, lacked consistency, and were difficult for non-designers to repurpose. Complex financial topics needed to be simplified while maintaining brand integrity.

My Role & Approach

Created a brand-consistent video system with modular templates, standardized typography, color, and animated graphics. Designed to be used by non-designers for new video topics.

Solution Overview

  • Modular narrative blocks (hook, concept, example, outcome)
  • Reusable motion graphics and lower-thirds
  • Editable templates for internal teams
  • Guidelines for maintaining visual and brand consistency

Impact & Results

  • Videos became consistent and brand-aligned
  • Non-designers could deploy and update content independently
  • Scalable system improved efficiency across campaigns

Key Highlights

Emphasizes brand consistency, operational efficiency, and scalable systems.
