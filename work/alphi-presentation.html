Client: Alphi PR · Service: Presentation Template System · Industry: PR / Communications

PR Firm Presentation Template

Alphi / New Deck

Repeatable presentation system with modular master slides for consistent client communications.

Branding · Template Design · Systems

Hero image

Context / Problem

A new PR firm needed professional, consistent decks that teams could quickly produce for client presentations. Existing methods were slow, inconsistent, and error-prone.

My Role & Approach

Designed a repeatable presentation system including master slides, content zones, and clear guidelines for text, imagery, and data visualization. Focused on non-designer usability.

Solution Overview

  • Modular master slide layouts
  • Template rules for typography, spacing, and imagery
  • Reusable charts and graphs
  • Guidelines for non-designers

Impact & Results

  • Reduced production time and errors
  • Enabled teams to deliver polished client decks independently
  • Strengthened brand consistency across all presentations

Key Highlights

Demonstrates repeatable systems, operational design, and non-designer enablement.
