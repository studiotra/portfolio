Client: TD Bank · Service: Cross-Channel Asset System · Industry: Financial Services / Education

TD Summer Reading Program

Cross-channel static and video assets that drove a 500% increase in program signups.

Template Design · Video · Print · Cross-Channel Systems

Hero image

Context / Problem

The TD Summer Reading Program needed a scalable set of assets for email, video, static graphics, and OOH campaigns. Previous campaigns were slow to produce and inconsistent across channels.

My Role & Approach

Built over 60 deliverables spanning static, video, and OOH formats. Created modular, reusable templates so non-designers could deploy assets across multiple channels efficiently.

Solution Overview

  • Modular video sequences and static graphics
  • Cross-channel consistency in typography, color, and iconography
  • Templates for quick adaptation across channels and specs

Impact & Results

  • Program signups increased by 500%
  • Streamlined production across static, video, and OOH assets
  • Brand consistency maintained across all channels

Key Highlights

Shows scalable templates, cross-channel systems thinking, and measurable business impact.
